                Barrington Brook, Erin Robinson & Catherine Robson

                Computer.play(Music, Creativity & Code) examines the creative potential of computer code in music and sound. The module looks at the fundamentals of computer programming, focusing on creative coding and themes related to computer music and interactivity. This event will showcase work by music students that have been exploring SuperCollider, a powerful programming language for the development of music and audio applications.
