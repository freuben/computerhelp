      Formative Task 4

      Read Chapter 1 (pp. 1-24) from Computer Music: Synthesis, Composition, and Performance (Dodge & Jerse 1997).

      SuperCollider IDE

      If from SuperCollider IDE, follow the instructions below.

      If you are on a Mac or Linux, evaluate the following line of code:

      "open http://www.squib-box.com/fr/SSfMT/Tasks/FundamentalsOfComputerMusic.pdf".unixCmd;

      If you are on Windows, evaluate the following line of code:

      "start http://www.squib-box.com/fr/SSfMT/Tasks/FundamentalsOfComputerMusic.pdf".unixCmd;
